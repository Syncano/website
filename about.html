Our Story

Syncano was created to help developers build, test, and ship amazing experiences on more devices, in less time, and with fewer resources. We simplify difficult and time-consuming engineering challenges, so you can have creative freedom and wow the world with what you build. We’re a small, international team dedicated to diversity and ready to provide support around the clock. We strive to break technological boundaries, fearlessly experiment to GSD (get stuff done), and be perfectly transparent both within our company and with you.
